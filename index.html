Hello, I’m Abhishek , based in New Delhi, I’m a 20-year-old finance student who lives for visual experimentation. For me, photography and filmmaking are all about narrative depth. I specialize in freezing the high-energy atmosphere of live events, capturing the real character behind a portrait, and documenting the world when I travel. I don't just want to take pictures , I want to create striking pieces of art that make you feel like you were standing right there with me.
